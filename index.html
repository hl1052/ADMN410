About us

We are one of the most professional pets' product shop in Durham area. We provide various types of pet supplies such as foods, apparels, and toys. All our products are manufactured by famous companies. They provide the best quality for these products. Also, we have three professional salesforces who have much expertise in pet product. If you are looking for pet’s supplies, Durham House of Pets (DHP) is the most convenient and inexpensive shop near you.

Our history

DHP was founded from a teen’s passion for keeping pets. In 1996, Andrew purchased a restaurant on Main Street, Durham. Then he converted the restaurant to a pets supplies shop. In 2000, Andrew met his new partners who are also passionate about pet product retailer as Andrew. They renovated the building to a fancy place. It is what you see now.
