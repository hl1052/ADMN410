Our Team




Andrew Young

Founder & CEO

Andrew is CEO of DHP. After graduation from the University of New Hampshire, he worked for a veterinarian hospital as an assistant. Then, he decided to work in the pet’s products industry and joined Petco as a product manager. Four years later, he quitted his job and founded DHP in 1996. He has worked for the pet product market for 20 years.
